About PARIKALAN

Parikalan is an organization that brings together individuals interested in computer science and related fields. The society's main aim is to foster collaboration, networking, learning, and sharing among its members. The society typically hosts various events, such as talks, workshops, hackathons, and competitions, that provide opportunities for members to enhance their technical skills, explore emerging technologies, and interact with professionals in the field. Moreover, the society may also engage in community outreach and social initiatives, using technology to solve real-world problems and make a positive impact on society. Overall, Parikalan serves as a hub for the exchange of knowledge and ideas in the field of computer science, enabling its members to stay up-to-date with the latest trends and developments, and to leverage their skills and expertise to drive innovation and progress.

RECRUITMENT Information

Forms are released in the beginning of every year for various positions at Coordinator, Sub-Coordinator and Member levels in each of the 6 clubs, namely, Codebots, Quizbots, Netweavers, EWS(Elocution, Workshops and Seminars)Club, RISE(Research, Innovate, Strategize and Explore)Club and GD(Graphic Designing)Club. Students can fill out the same depending on their level of interest. There is as such no limit to the number of clubs, one wants to work with. After registration, each mentor takes personal interviews and an assessment is given to all the participants in order to scrutinize deserving peers for being a part of the society and contribute to its welfare.
