E-Tambola

Match numbers on your digital card against an algorithm that will have you shouting "Tambola!" in no time! Participants are divided in teams and they are assigned Bingo/Tambola E-Tickets.
The facilitator calls the cue number one at a time and team members respond as quickly as possible to strike off the numbers and claim the winning prizes.

📆 15th April 2023
🕑 01:00pm-02:15pm

Rules

  1. This is a solo event.
  2. The event will consist of two rounds, Preliminary and Mains.
  3. We have made a code that randomly selects a topic for the participant.
  4. Participant will be given 150 seconds in the prelims and 80 seconds in mains to describe the topic in his words.
  5. Using words and phrases which hurt the sentiments of any religion, caste and race will not be tolerated. The participant will be eliminated on the spot.
  6. As soon as the clock starts for the participant, he/she has to speak about the positives of the given topic. After exactly half time (75 seconds in prelims and 40 seconds in mains) a bell will ring which means now the participant has to speak about the negative of the topic.
  7. The decision of judges will be final and binding.

FAQs

What is XENIUM?

Xenium, the yearly event organized by the Computer Science Society of PGDAV College, Delhi University, is a thrilling extravaganza that showcases various competitions at the college level. With a generous prize pool, it's an excellent opportunity for students to test their skills and win big! Join us for an unforgettable experience.

How can I register for the events and what is the fee?

Every event above on the page consists a register button. Click it to register. NO FEE WILL BE CHARGED!!!!!!

What kind of prizes can I win at XENIUM?

Prizes for XENIUM vary depending on the event and competition. Some events offer cash prizes, while others may offer tech gadgets or other valuable items.

Are there any prerequisites or requirements for attending XENIUM?

Some events may require certain technical skills or knowledge, while others may be open to anyone interested in technology. Be sure to check the event details before registering.
